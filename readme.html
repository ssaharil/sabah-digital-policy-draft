Sabah State Digital Policy

This folder contains the full draft manuscript of the Sabah Digital Government Innovation (SDGI) policy document, structured as nine sequential chapters covering the vision, strategy, governance, and implementation roadmap for Sabah's digital transformation.

Draft — Not for Public Distribution

Overview

The Sabah State Digital Policy articulates the state's commitment to advancing digital transformation under the SDGI Roadmap 2020–2030. The overarching journey is framed as a progression: Automation → Innovation → Intelligence, with the ultimate goal of building a data-driven, citizen-centric government guided by the 5A principle — Accessible, by Anyone, at Anytime, Anywhere, and by Any Device.

The policy draws on global and regional digital transformation benchmarks, assesses Sabah's current digital landscape, and charts a strategic course across multiple thrusts — from data integration infrastructure and AI adoption to citizen engagement and open government data.

Editing workflow: The 9 HTML files in the root folder are the working documents. To make changes, copy the relevant file into digitalpolicy_draft/, edit the copy there, and log the change in digitalpolicy_change_log.html. Never edit the root HTML files directly.

Document Structure

1
Preface
Foreword from the Chief Minister and State Secretary of Sabah, setting out the political commitment to digital transformation and the 5A vision.
2
Perspectives on Digital Transformation
Global and regional context — how leading nations and Malaysia's federal government are approaching digital transformation, providing the benchmarks that inform Sabah's strategy.
3
The Landscape of Digital Transformation in Sabah
Historical evolution from early ICT adoption in the late 1990s to the current SDGI initiatives, highlighting key milestones, challenges, and the present state of digital infrastructure in Sabah.
4
Sabah State Digital Policy
The core policy statement — vision, mission, guiding principles, and strategic objectives under the SDGI initiative, including achievements from the 2020–2024 phase and the direction for 2025–2030.
5
Thrusts, Strategies, and Initiatives
Detailed breakdown of strategic thrusts: Data Integration Platform (X-Road), Data Hubs (People, Business, Geo), Digital ID (Sabah ePass), AI adoption, cybersecurity, open data, and smart infrastructure — each with specific strategies and initiatives.
6
Governance Structure
Defines the institutional framework — the Digital Government Division under the Sabah State Public Service Department, inter-agency coordination mechanisms, and accountability structures for policy implementation.
7
Citizen Engagement Strategy
A comprehensive strategy to build public awareness, trust, and adoption of digital government services — with targeted outreach for underserved and rural communities across Sabah.
8
Closing
Concluding remarks on Sabah's digital future — the shared responsibility of government, citizens, and the private sector in realising the SDGI 2020–2030 vision.
9
Definition and Glossary
Standardised definitions for key terms used throughout the policy — AI, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Data Hub, Digital ID, Open Data, X-Road, and more.
