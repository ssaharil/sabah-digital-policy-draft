8. Closing

Rujukan: JPANS/BKD/SDGI/008 Versi 1.0 (Draf) SDGI Roadmap 2020–2030

Sabah stands at the threshold of a new digital era — one that promises greater efficiency, transparency, inclusivity, and innovation in governance. The journey from automation to intelligence has laid a solid foundation, but the path ahead demands commitment, collaboration, and courage. As we move forward under the SDGI Roadmap 2020–2030, our focus must remain unwavering: to build a government that serves every Sabahan with integrity, foresight, and empathy.

The successful implementation of this digital policy hinges not only on technology but also on people — the civil servants who will lead change, the citizens who will embrace it, and the businesses and communities that will benefit from it. By fostering a culture of continuous learning, open data, and ethical AI, Sabah can become a model for smart and sustainable digital transformation in Malaysia and beyond.
