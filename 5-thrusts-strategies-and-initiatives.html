Thrusts, Strategies,
and Initiatives

Sabah State Digital Policy JPANS/BKD/SDGI/005 Jan 2025

This chapter outlines the five strategic thrusts that define Sabah's digital transformation roadmap under the SDGI initiative. Each thrust is supported by a targeted strategy and a set of concrete initiatives designed to move Sabah progressively from automation and digitalisation toward intelligence-driven governance.

Thrust 1 Data Integration and Interchange Platform

Strategy: Implement a data integration and interchange platform using X-Road technology to enable seamless data sharing across government agencies.

Initiatives
  • Develop and deploy the X-Road platform for secure data exchange
  • Establish data governance frameworks to ensure data security and privacy
  • Train government personnel on data integration and management
Thrust 2 Data Hubs (People Hub, Business Hub, Geo Hub)

Strategy: Create centralised data hubs to consolidate and manage citizen, business, and geospatial data.

Initiatives
  • Develop the People Hub to centralise citizen data for better service delivery
  • Establish the Business Hub to streamline business-related data and services
  • Launch the Geo Hub to manage geospatial data for urban planning and disaster management
Thrust 3 Sabah Open Data Initiative

Strategy: Promote transparency and innovation by making government data publicly accessible.

Initiatives
  • Launch the Sabah Open Data Portal to provide access to government datasets
  • Encourage the use of open data by researchers, businesses, and the public
  • Organise hackathons and innovation challenges to drive data-driven solutions

Thrust 4 Modernisation of District Offices and Public Service Department

Strategy: Upgrade district offices and public service departments with digital tools and platforms.

Initiatives
  • Implement digital workflows and automation in district offices
  • Provide training for public servants on digital tools and platforms
  • Develop a centralised portal for online public services
Thrust 5 Smart City Initiatives

Strategy: Develop smart city projects in selected districts to improve urban living and sustainability.

Initiatives
  • Implement smart traffic management systems to reduce congestion
  • Deploy IoT sensors for environmental monitoring (air quality, waste management)
  • Develop smart public transportation systems to enhance mobility
  • Promote citizen engagement through smart city apps and platforms

Summary: Thrusts at a Glance

# Thrust Strategic Focus Key Initiatives
1 Data Integration & Interchange Platform X-Road technology for secure inter-agency data exchange X-Road deployment, data governance, personnel training
2 Data Hubs Centralised management of citizen, business & geospatial data People Hub, Business Hub, Geo Hub
3 Sabah Open Data Initiative Transparency and innovation through public data access Open Data Portal, hackathons, innovation challenges
4 Modernisation of District Offices Digital workflows and capacity building in public service Digital workflows, staff training, centralised service portal
5 Smart City Initiatives Technology-driven urban living and sustainability Smart traffic, IoT sensors, public transport, citizen apps
Together, these five thrusts form a coherent, layered strategy — from building the data infrastructure and opening government data, to modernising frontline services and creating intelligent, liveable urban environments for all Sabahans.
